Country Style

Country interior design represents a casual and cosy decorating style, which eludes to the feeling of a simple country life. The colour palette incorporates pastels and bright colours.

Our wood and wood-look flooring ranges provide infinite options and inspiration to help you find the perfect wood floor for your country styled room.

Modern Flat

Modern Flat design is an interior design style characterized by a monochromatic color palette, clean lines, minimalism, natural materials, and natural light.

Modern Flat design involves being mindful of lighting, planning storage, color blocking & zoning and layering in such a way that the apartment is not cluttered and has a stylish punch.

Stylish Office

Our concept of Stylish offices involves focusing more on spatial organization rather than cubical office displays. The design approach merely leans towards the idea of increasing creative energy in the working professionals.

The use of colours and intrinsic details play a major role in designing the corners of the office space with interior sliding doors.
